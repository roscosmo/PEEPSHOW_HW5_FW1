# Transformation And Form System

The slime does not evolve conventionally.

It adapts, imitates, absorbs, mutates, and expresses traits.

## Adaptation Instead Of Evolution

Forms should feel like ecological identity and relationship history, not a linear upgrade tree.

The slime may copy nearby creature traits, express environmental pressure, or mutate through ritual context.

## Fake Evolution Rituals

The game may imitate classic evolution ceremonies, then subvert them.

The lesson is:

"This creature does not evolve normally."

## Forms As Identity

Forms affect:

- traversal
- social access
- emotional tendencies
- environmental preference
- pet care
- combat behavior
- NPC interpretation

Example form directions:

- cat
- vampire
- cactus
- fungal
- aquatic
- armored
- shadow
- dream

## Environmental Influence

Transformation pressure can emerge from:

- light exposure
- nighttime play
- stillness
- movement habits
- emotional routines
- rituals
- environmental conditions

Examples:

- bright stillness -> cactus tendency
- late-night routines -> nocturnal tendency
- darkness -> vampire tendency

## Forms As Social Identity

Forms determine who accepts the slime, who fears it, and what spaces it can enter.

Examples:

- cat form may access private domestic spaces
- vampire form may be accepted at occult rituals
- fungal form may be welcomed underground

## System Rule

Forms are not merely abilities.

They are social, emotional, ecological, and tactical states.

# Death And Resurrection Ritual

True slime death should be extremely rare.

It should likely occur once, during a major narrative rupture caused by an overwhelmingly powerful event or entity.

## Death State

After death, the device should feel empty:

- no blinking
- no idle movement
- no wheel response
- no reaction
- silence
- no casual pet simulation behavior

The absence is emotional content.

## Resurrection Discovery

The player may remember:

- midnight rituals
- occult gatherings
- forbidden spaces
- NPC routines
- strange prior dialogue
- ritual symbols or audio patterns

The intended realization is:

"Maybe they can bring the slime back."

## Ritual Requirements

The resurrection should occur at actual midnight or a tightly defined real-time ritual window.

Possible realtime interaction ingredients:

- rotary rune tracing
- IMU stabilization
- timing interactions
- light shielding or exposure
- rhythm matching
- occult BBB/audio patterns
- large face-on ritual art

## Consequence

The slime returns recognizable but subtly changed.

The emotional question becomes:

"Is this still my friend?"

Continuity is rediscovered through familiar behaviors, eyes, timing, and relationship memory.
